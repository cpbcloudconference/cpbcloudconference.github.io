CPB Cloud Conference

Hosted by 153 CPT

About the Conference

The CPB's first ever Cloud Conference will educate soldiers on what the cloud is and how to hunt in the cloud with a focus on Microsoft Azure.

Date and Time

The Conference will take place at the Georgia Cyber Center on January 20th, 2022. The event will start at 0830 and end at 1700. The conference will be streamed over M Microsoft teams and held in person. In person attendance is limited to the first 200 attendees

FAQ

  • Will I need to bring my own Devices?
    • Yes! You will need your own devices in order to follow along with the lectures and the hands on portion.
